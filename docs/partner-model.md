# Partner Model

## Definition

The Autonomyx Partner Delivery Network is a commercial ecosystem for delivering open source agentic infrastructure with trust, governance, and operational accountability.

Autonomyx provides the foundation, standards, architecture, certification, and marketplace trust layer. Partners provide implementation, integration, operations, training, and industry specialization.

## Core thesis

Open source creates adoption. Commercial delivery creates confidence. Partner networks create scale.

Autonomyx combines all three:

```text
Open Source Software
+ Commercial Delivery
+ Certified Partner Network
+ Governed Marketplace
= Open Source Commercial Core
```

## What Autonomyx owns

- Platform direction
- Reference architecture
- Partner standards
- Certification system
- Marketplace governance
- Trust score model
- Conformance requirements
- Brand usage rules
- Commercial packaging
- Ecosystem quality control

- Customer discovery
- Deployment

- Training
- Customer success

## Partner categories

### Implementation Partner

Deploys Autonomyx into customer environments.

Typical work:

- Platform installation
- Tenant setup
- SSO and identity configuration
- Data source registration
- Agent and workflow configuration
- Go-live support

### Managed Service Partner

Runs Autonomyx for customers after deployment.

Typical work:

- Monitoring
- Backup
- Upgrade management
- Incident response
- Cost controls
- Reliability review
- Security patching

### Solution Partner

Builds industry or functional solutions on Autonomyx.

Typical assets:

- Industry agents
- Workflow templates
- Policy packs
- Connectors
- Dashboards
- Evaluation packs

### Technology Partner

Provides infrastructure, integrations, or services that connect to Autonomyx.

Typical assets:

- Certified connector
- Reference deployment
- Joint architecture
- Integration guide

### Training Partner

Delivers enablement and certification preparation.

Typical work:

- Admin training
- Operator training
- Builder training
- Governance training
- Industry enablement

## Operating principle

Partners are not resellers only. Partners are accountable delivery owners.

Every partner must prove capability through evidence, not claims.
